GETTING YOUR HEAD STRAIGHT

Spider-Man Stress Relief

Web your worries away with these stress coping tips!

1. Embrace Your Spidey Senses

Take a few moments each day to tune into your senses. Embrace your inner Spidey and be present in the moment.

2. Power Naps

Ensure you get power naps to recharge your superhero abilities. Even Spider-Man needs a break to save the day!

3. Swing Through Stress

Imagine yourself swinging through the city like Spider-Man. Visualizing positive scenarios can help reduce stress and anxiety.

4. Spidey Breath

Practice deep breathing exercises. Inhale deeply, imagining you're filling up like Spider-Man's lungs, then exhale slowly to release stress.
